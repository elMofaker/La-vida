About the hotel

The Max Hotel is one of the best hotels in the world, offering you a unique experience with all the luxurious facilities you need for a comfortable stay.

An unforgettable luxury experience

Enjoy a luxurious stay in the heart of the city where you find luxury and comfort around every corner. Our hotel offers you five-star service in a sophisticated atmosphere, making every moment you spend with us unforgettable.

Our excellent location

Our excellent location in the heart of the city guarantees you access to the most prominent tourist attractions, shops and restaurants. The luxury hotel offers you all modern amenities, from elegant rooms to distinguished services.

Services and facilities

We offer you a range of integrated services such as spa services, fitness facilities, and fine dining to ensure your comfort throughout your stay. Enjoy the highest level of services, from 24-hour room service, to organizing tours and excursions.

Ideal stay for families

Our hotel is the ideal choice for families looking for comfort and fun. With many activities and special offers for children, we offer you an unforgettable family experience.

Luxury around every corner

Every corner of the luxury hotel is specially designed to provide an exceptional luxury experience, from the spacious rooms to the world-class customer service. We are here to provide you with a hotel experience that exceeds all your expectations.
